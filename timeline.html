⏳ Timeline

From Debut to Deadline — A Decade of History

2016
Aug 2016
🌸 BLACKPINK Debuts
YG Entertainment's new girl group makes their explosive debut with 'Whistle' and 'Boombayah'. The world instantly takes notice as both tracks dominate Korean charts.
Nov 2016
🏆 1st Music Show Win
BLACKPINK wins their first music show award with 'Playing With Fire' — an emotional milestone for the rookie group, proving they are not just a flash in the pan.
2018
Jun 2018
💜 Square Up Mini Album
Release of their landmark 1st mini album featuring 'DDU-DU DDU-DU'. The MV breaks YouTube records immediately, becoming the fastest K-pop video to reach 100M views at the time.
Nov 2018
💎 Jennie Solo Debut
Jennie becomes the first BLACKPINK member to debut as a soloist with 'SOLO'. The track peaks at #1 on multiple Korean charts and showcases Jennie's iconic stage presence.
2019
Apr 2019
❤️ Kill This Love EP
The 'Kill This Love' EP drops and sets a new YouTube 24-hour record for K-pop with 56.7M views. The title track becomes one of their most iconic anthems with its brass-heavy drop.
Apr 2019
🎪 Coachella Performance
BLACKPINK makes history as the first K-pop act ever to perform at Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival, cementing their status as truly global superstars.
2020
Jan 2020
🎤 In Your Area — The Show
BLACKPINK's first-ever solo concert experience, 'The Show', captivates fans as they perform with full production. A landmark moment ahead of the album era.
Oct 2020
🌟 THE ALBUM Drops
Their first full-length album debuts with 'Lovesick Girls' as title track. 'Ice Cream' (feat. Selena Gomez) becomes a global smash. 'How You Like That' breaks 3 Guinness World Records.
2021
Feb 2021
🌹 Rosé Solo Debut
Rosé releases her debut solo album '-R-' featuring 'On The Ground' and 'Gone'. 'On The Ground' tops charts in multiple countries and showcases Rosé's soulful, emotional artistry.
Sep 2021
👑 Lisa Solo Debut
'Lalisa' and 'Money' break YouTube records simultaneously. 'Lalisa' becomes one of the fastest K-pop solos to hit 100M views. Lisa's charisma and dance prowess captivate the world.
2022
Sep 2022
🩷 Born Pink Era Begins
BLACKPINK announces their highly-anticipated comeback with 'Pink Venom'. The pre-release single debuts at #22 on Billboard Hot 100, their highest chart position at the time.
Oct 2022
🌍 Born Pink World Tour
The Born Pink World Tour kicks off — the biggest K-pop girl group world tour at the time. BLACKPINK sells out stadiums across Asia, Europe, North America, and beyond.
2023
Mar 2023
🌸 Jisoo Solo Debut
Jisoo drops 'Flower (꽃)', and the world collectively loses its mind. The song becomes an instant phenomenon, cementing Jisoo's status as a powerhouse solo artist under her label BLISSOO.
2024
Jun 2024
🎸 Lisa 'Rockstar'
Lisa drops the banger 'Rockstar' with her new independent label LLOUD. The track goes viral overnight and shows a bolder, grittier side of Lisa as a solo artist.
Oct 2024
🍎 Rosé & Bruno Mars — APT.
Rosé collaborates with Bruno Mars on 'APT.' and the result is a global smash hit. The track tops charts in dozens of countries and becomes one of 2024's biggest songs worldwide.
Nov 2024
✍️ Contracts Renewed!
All four BLACKPINK members renew their group contracts with YG Entertainment, ending months of fan speculation. The renewal signals a new era of group activities while maintaining solo ventures.
2026
Jan 2026
⚡ JUMP — Pre-Release
BLACKPINK drops 'JUMP' as a teaser to the new era, sending Blinks into a frenzy. The energetic pre-release single showcases a more mature, experimental sound from the group.
Feb 2026
🌌 Deadline Era — 'GO' ft. Chris Martin
'GO' drops from their 3rd mini album 'Deadline' — a historic EDM/techno anthem written by all four members alongside Coldplay's Chris Martin. A truly once-in-a-generation collab.
2026
🗺️ Deadline World Tour
The massively successful Deadline World Tour wraps, solidifying BLACKPINK as the biggest girl group on the planet. A decade after debut, they stand at the absolute peak of their powers.
