🥇 Guinness World Records
🏅 Guinness World Record
'How You Like That' — Most Viewed in 24hrs
Set the record for the most viewed YouTube music video within the first 24 hours of release, achieving over 86.3 million views. Also broke 3 separate Guinness Records simultaneously.
🏅 Guinness World Record
'Kill This Love' — Most Viewed in 24hrs
Previously held the record for the most viewed YouTube MV in 24 hours for a K-pop act, smashing records at the time of release in 2019 with 56.7M views in 24 hours.
🏅 Guinness World Record
First K-pop Group — 3 MVs with 1B+ Views
Became the first music group from South Korea — and first K-pop act of any kind — to have three separate music videos each surpass one billion views on YouTube.
🏅 Guinness World Record
Most Instagram Followers — Music Group
Held the Guinness record for the most followed music group on Instagram, surpassing 60 million followers on their official @blackpinkofficial account at the time of the record.
🏅 Guinness World Record
Most Subscribed YouTube Channel — Girl Group
Recognized as the most subscribed YouTube music channel for any girl group globally, with their official channel amassing tens of millions of subscribers worldwide.
📈 Billboard Milestones
Billboard Hot 100
First K-pop Girl Group on Hot 100
BLACKPINK made history as the first K-pop girl group to chart on the legendary Billboard Hot 100, breaking barriers for Korean artists worldwide.
Billboard Hot 100
3 Songs Simultaneously on Hot 100
Became the first K-pop act to simultaneously have three different songs chart on the Billboard Hot 100, cementing their global dominance.
Hot 100 Peak
'Pink Venom' Debuted at #22
'Pink Venom' debuted at #22 on the Billboard Hot 100, marking a career-best chart position at the time and proving their ever-growing US fanbase.
