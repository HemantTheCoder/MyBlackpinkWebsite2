About Blackpink

Blackpink (Hangul: 블랙핑크; commonly stylized as BLACKPINK or BLΛƆKPIИK) is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The group debuted in August 2016 with their single album Square One, which featured "Whistle" and "Boombayah", their first number-one entries on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart and the Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart, respectively.

Blackpink has broken numerous online records throughout their career. Their music videos for "Kill This Love" (2019) and "How You Like That" each set records for the most-viewed music video within the first 24 hours of release, with the latter breaking three and setting two Guinness World Records. They are also the first music group and Korean act to have three music videos with at least one billion views on YouTube.

Recent Activities (2025-2026): Blackpink recently completed their massively successful "Deadline" World Tour in January 2026. Following their 2025 pre-release single "JUMP", they released their highly anticipated third mini-album, Deadline, in February 2026. While the members renewed their group contracts with YG Entertainment, they have all established their own successful agencies for solo activities: Jennie (OA/ODD ATELIER), Lisa (LLOUD), Jisoo (BLISSOO), and Rosé (signed with THEBLACKLABEL).
